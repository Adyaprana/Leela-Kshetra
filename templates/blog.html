The Significance of Rath Yatra

Rath Yatra, also known as the Festival of Chariots, is one of the most famous Hindu festivals. Celebrated primarily in Odisha, it symbolizes the journey of Lord Jagannath, Lord Balabhadra, and Devi Subhadra from their temple to Gundicha Mandir. This auspicious event not only unites devotees in faith and joy but also spreads the message of equality as everyone is welcome to pull the sacred chariots.

The Blessing of Prasadam

In Hindu temples, Prasadam signifies more than food. It is a divine offering made to the deities and then distributed among devotees as a token of blessings. Receiving Prasadam is not just about nourishment; it is a moment of spiritual connection with the divine grace of the Lord.
