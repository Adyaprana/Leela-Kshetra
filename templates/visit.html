Visitor Guidelines

Dress Code

For Men:

  • Dhoti or traditional Indian attire preferred
  • Kurta-pajama in light colors
  • Modest clothing covering shoulders and knees

For Women:

  • Saree or salwar kameez preferred
  • Modest clothing with covered shoulders
  • Traditional Indian attire recommended

Photography

  • Photography is allowed in designated areas
  • Flash photography prohibited near deities
  • Respect other devotees while taking photos
  • Commercial photography requires permission

Temple Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering temple premises
  • Maintain silence in prayer areas
  • Follow the queue system during aarti
  • Keep mobile phones on silent mode
  • Do not touch the deities or offerings

Offerings

  • Flowers, fruits, and sweets are welcome
  • Coconut and incense sticks available at temple
  • Monetary donations accepted in donation box
  • Special puja arrangements can be made
