Spiritual Services We Offer

Our temple provides a comprehensive range of spiritual services designed to support your spiritual growth and connect you with the divine. Each service is conducted with devotion and traditional authenticity.

Daily Puja Services

Experience the divine through our daily puja rituals including morning and evening aarti, abhishek, and special prayers.

Timings:

  • Morning Aarti: 6:00 AM - 7:00 AM
  • Abhishek: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Evening Aarti: 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Havan & Yagna

Sacred fire ceremonies for purification, blessings, and spiritual upliftment. Special havans for festivals and personal occasions.

Available Services:

  • Ganapati Havan
  • Navagraha Havan
  • Mahamrityunjaya Havan
  • Satyanarayan Puja

Wedding Ceremonies

Sacred Hindu wedding ceremonies conducted according to traditional Vedic rituals in our beautiful temple setting.

Services Include:

  • Pre-wedding consultations
  • Vedic ceremony guidance
  • Priest services
  • Temple venue booking

Naming Ceremonies

Traditional Namkaran ceremonies to bless newborns with auspicious names according to Vedic traditions and astrological guidance.

Ceremony Includes:

  • Astrological name selection
  • Sacred rituals
  • Blessing ceremonies
  • Certificate provision

Spiritual Education

Learn from sacred texts including Bhagavad Gita, Ramayana, and other spiritual literature with experienced teachers.

Classes Available:

  • Bhagavad Gita: Sundays 10:00 AM
  • Sanskrit Classes: Saturdays 4:00 PM
  • Meditation: Daily 6:30 AM
  • Youth Programs: Saturdays 5:00 PM

Spiritual Counseling

Spiritual guidance and counseling services to help navigate life's challenges with wisdom, faith, and divine support.

Available Sessions:

  • Individual counseling
  • Family guidance
  • Grief support
  • Life transition guidance

Special Occasions & Festivals

Festival Celebrations

Grand celebrations of major Hindu festivals including Diwali, Holi, Janmashtami, and Navratri with traditional rituals and community participation.

Kirtan & Bhajan

Devotional singing sessions featuring traditional bhajans and kirtans that uplift the spirit and create divine atmosphere.

Prasadam Service

Sacred food offerings blessed by the deities and distributed to devotees as divine grace and community sharing.

Community Service

Regular community service activities including food drives, educational programs, and support for those in need.
