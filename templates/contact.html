Frequently Asked Questions

What are the temple visiting hours?

Our temple is open daily from 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM in the morning and 4:00 PM to 9:00 PM in the evening. Special extended hours during festivals and important occasions.

Is there a dress code for visiting the temple?

We encourage modest, traditional attire. For men: dhoti, kurta-pajama, or modest clothing. For women: saree, salwar kameez, or modest clothing with covered shoulders. Please remove shoes before entering.

Can I book special puja services?

Yes, we offer various puja services including Satyanarayan Puja, Havan ceremonies, and special occasion pujas. Please contact us in advance to book and discuss requirements.

Is parking available at the temple?

Yes, we provide free parking facilities for both cars and two-wheelers. During festivals, additional parking arrangements are made to accommodate more visitors.

How can I make donations to the temple?

Donations can be made through our online donation form, bank transfer, or in person at the temple. All donations are tax-deductible and receipts are provided.

Do you offer spiritual education classes?

Yes, we conduct regular classes on Bhagavad Gita, Sanskrit, meditation, and youth programs. Check our events page for current schedules and registration details.
