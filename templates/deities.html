Lord Jagannath & Family

Lord Jagannath

Jagannath is a Hindu deity, considered an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, revered in Odisha, India. His annual Rath Yatra festival is a major pilgrimage. The name means "Lord of the Universe"; also known as Mahaprabhu, the "Great Lord."

Lord Balabhadra

White-colored brother of Lord Jagannath, symbolizing strength and power. He represents the divine strength that supports and protects devotees.

Subhadra

Yellow-colored sister of Lord Jagannath, symbolizes compassion and auspiciousness. She represents divine feminine energy and motherly love.

Sudarshan

The divine weapon of Lord Vishnu, represents justice and protection. It symbolizes the power that destroys evil and protects righteousness.

Other Sacred Deities

Lord Shiva

Mahadev, the destroyer and transformer within the Hindu trinity. Often depicted with a serpent around his neck and holding a trident.

Lord Ganesha

The elephant-headed god, revered as the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune.

Maa Durga

Powerful goddess depicted with multiple arms wielding weapons, symbolizing divine feminine energy that overcomes evil.

Radha Krishna

Eternal divine couple symbolizing divine love, devotion, and the soul's yearning for union with the Supreme.

Maa Lakshmi

Goddess of wealth, fortune, and prosperity. Depicted with lotus flowers and coins symbolizing abundance.

Lord Hanuman

Devotee of Lord Rama, symbol of strength, devotion, and courage. Known for his unwavering faith and immense power.

Maa Saraswati

Goddess of wisdom, music, and learning. Often shown with a veena and white swan, symbolizing purity and knowledge.

Sri Ram & Sita

Divine couple representing the ideals of dharma, devotion, and marital virtue. Central figures of the epic Ramayana.

🌌 Lord Vishnu

The preserver in the Hindu trinity. Protector of the universe who incarnates in many forms to restore cosmic balance.

🗡️ Lord Kartikeya

Warrior god and son of Lord Shiva and Parvati. Known as Murugan, he symbolizes valor, youth, and divine leadership.

🐂 Nandi

The sacred bull and devoted gatekeeper of Lord Shiva. Symbolizes strength, loyalty, and dharma in Hindu tradition.

🦅 Garuda

The mighty eagle and vehicle of Lord Vishnu. Symbol of speed, strength, and divine service, Garuda is often worshipped for protection from evil and poison.
